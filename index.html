Our idea

We’re creating the first-ever strategy-narrative game where the life of an ant colony becomes a dynamic, real-time story – narrated by a professional voice actor in the style of Discovery documentaries.

The player manages the colony: gathering resources, defending against predators, building structures, expanding the population, and adapting to an ever-changing environment.

Ant paths, realistic threats, smart RTS mechanics, and immersive narration combine into an experience where gameplay becomes a living story – meant to be played and remembered.
